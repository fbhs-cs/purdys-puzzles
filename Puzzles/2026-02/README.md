# February 2026 Puzzle

```
If you write out dates using mmddyyyy format, February 1, 2026 would
be written 02012026.  The proper factors of 2,012,026 are 1, 2, 73, 146, 13781, 27562, 1006013.
Adding up these factors gives 1,047,578, which is less than 2,012,026.  Therefore
2,012,026 is called a "deficient" number.  February 3, 2026, or 2,032,026 has 15 factors
whose sum is 2,050,374.  Since the sum of the proper factors of 2,032,026 is more than
2,032,026, 2,032,026 is called an "abundant" number.  If you write out all of the dates
in February of this year in the shown format, there are 19 deficient numbers and 9 abundant numbers.
How many deficient and abunandant numbers will result when you write out every date
in 2026?
```
